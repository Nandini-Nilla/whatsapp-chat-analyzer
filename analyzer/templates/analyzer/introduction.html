Welcome to WhatsApp Analyzer

This tool helps you analyze your WhatsApp chat data. Gain insights into your messaging habits, visualize your activity, and discover trends. Simply upload your chat file and let the magic happen!

About the Project

WhatsApp Analyzer is a powerful tool designed to help you understand your WhatsApp chat data better. Whether you're looking to analyze your personal conversations or gain insights into group chats, this tool provides a comprehensive analysis of your messaging patterns.

With WhatsApp Analyzer, you can:

  • Track your most active days and times.
  • Identify your most frequently used words and emojis.
  • Visualize your chat activity with interactive graphs.
  • Analyze sentiment trends over time.

How It Works

Using WhatsApp Analyzer is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Export Your Chat: Open WhatsApp, go to the chat you want to analyze, and export the chat without media.
  2. Upload the File: Click the "Get Started" button below and upload your exported chat file.
  3. Choose Device: Select The Format like Android and Ios.
  4. Analyze and Explore: Sit back and let WhatsApp Analyzer process your data. Explore the insights and visualizations!
